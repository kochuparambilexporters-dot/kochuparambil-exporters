Our History

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between local quality and global demand, Kochuparambil Exporters began its journey as a specialized logistics partner in Kerala. Over the years, we have evolved into a premier export house, streamlining the supply chain for premium produce and essential commodities.

Our commitment to transparency and time-sensitive delivery has allowed us to expand our footprint from India to international hubs in Europe and beyond, ensuring that excellence knows no borders.

How It Works

1

Choose Your Service

Select the logistics solution that fits your timeline and cargo type. Whether you are moving perishables or bulk commodities, we have a specialized route for you.

  • Customized Export Plans: Tailored to your specific volume and destination.
  • Global Destination Reach: Shipping to major ports and airports worldwide.
  • Expert Consultation: Professional advice on international trade regulations.
2

Select Your Payment

We provide secure and flexible payment options to ensure a smooth transaction process. Our financial workflows are designed to accommodate international trade standards.

  • Secure Transactions: Encrypted processing for all financial transfers.
  • Flexible Terms: Options for various Letters of Credit and wire transfers.
  • Transparent Invoicing: No hidden fees; clear, itemized billing for every shipment
3

Tracking Your Order

Stay informed with real-time updates. Once your shipment departs, you can monitor its progress from our facility in Kerala all the way to its final destination.

  • Real-Time Monitoring: 24/7 visibility into your cargo's current location.
  • Status Notifications: Instant alerts via email or SMS at key milestones
  • Delivery Confirmation: Digital proof of delivery provided upon arrival.
